Features

Everything your team's rituals need. Nothing they don't.

For teams tired of juggling multiple tools, Huddl brings boards, retrospectives, and collaboration into a single, beautifully simple platform.

Circles

Organize teams, not chaos. Create Circles to group your people, then share boards with exactly who needs them. Circle Leads control access; members collaborate freely.

Ongoing Huddls

Persistent Kanban-style boards for continuous work — backlogs, roadmaps, and ongoing projects. Start from Kanban and Brainstorm templates, or build from scratch.

Session Huddls

Time-based retrospective boards with built-in history. Schedule sessions weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — every session is preserved so insights never get lost.

Real-time collaboration

See who's viewing every board, watch updates land instantly, and collaborate live without conflicts. Presence indicators keep the whole team in sync.

Ready-made templates

Kanban, Retro, Brainstorm, or Blank — pick a layout and your board is ready before the meeting starts. Rich text cards, drag-and-drop, comments, and archiving built in.

Cloud or local — your call

Sync across devices with secure cloud mode, or keep every byte in your browser with local mode. Either way, import your Trello boards in one click.

FAQ

Questions? Huddl up.

What makes Huddl different from Trello or Miro?

Huddl combines Trello's board simplicity with purpose-built retrospectives — no tool sprawl. Circles give you real team-level organization that Trello lacks, and Huddl stays focused on team ceremonies instead of Miro's infinite (and often overwhelming) canvas.

Is Huddl really free?

Yes. Local mode is free forever — all your data lives in your browser, with unlimited boards and templates. When your team wants real-time collaboration and cross-device sync, upgrade to Cloud.

What's the difference between Ongoing and Session Huddls?

Ongoing Huddls are persistent boards for continuous work like backlogs and roadmaps. Session Huddls are time-based boards for recurring rituals like retrospectives — each session is saved to history, so past insights are always a click away.

Can I import my existing Trello boards?

Yes — Huddl imports from Trello in one click, so your team can migrate without losing work or momentum.

How does Huddl handle security and privacy?

Cloud mode uses Google sign-in and stores data on Google Firebase infrastructure. Prefer maximum privacy? Local mode keeps every byte in your browser — nothing ever leaves your device.

Does Huddl work on mobile?

Huddl runs in any modern browser and is fully responsive, so it works on tablets and phones as well as desktops.
